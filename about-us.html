Why Should I Choose Prestige?

At Prestige the most important person in our office is you, our patient. We work as a team to provide the highest level of Orthodontic care and customer service possible.

Synergy R System (SWLF)

At Prestige, we use the Synergy R System (SWLF) from USA. This innovative braces system works just like Damon braces but at a lower price! You will see a better smile faster, with less discomfort, and even need fewer visits to the Orthodontist.

Why Should I Choose Prestige?


Because Dr. Chay is a recognized Orthodontic Specialist by the Ministry of Health – National Specialist Register, having your braces treatment at Prestige is the best way to make sure you get a great smile using the latest methods. Don’t settle for a general dentist with little or no braces training for your dental braces care. Some general dentists start to apply braces after only a short weekend course of training. Correcting tooth and jaw alignment problems is a complex process, and is best handled by a proper Masters Degree qualified Orthodontist, such as Dr. Chay.

Dr. Chay

Dr. Chay is a true Orthodontic specialist, who received her Masters in Orthodontics from the University of Hong Kong in 1999.
