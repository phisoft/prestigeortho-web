Financing

The cost of your orthodontic treatment varies depends on your individual needs and treatment plan. Dr Chay will discuss with you the treatment plan and the payment to fit your expectations and budget before you begin treatment.
At Prestige, paying for your braces care is easy. We offer a no-interest installment plan.
Mode of payment is cash, cheque, credit card or debit card.
